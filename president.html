From President's Pen ...

Sai Ram!

My humble Pranams to our beloved Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba. "Mother is every man’s primary wealth. There is no higher God than the mother. The father is one’s treasure. Instead of valuing such precious wealth and divinity, men engage themselves in acquiring all kinds of riches and possessions. Everyone should cherish one’s parents as embodiments of the Divine. The mother and father may be physically separate, but spiritually they are one. One must love and serve them. An ungrateful person is worse than a beast.” Baba

Our Fathers day celebration took a very welcoming twist this year at our Edmonton Center. Our Young Adults, in partnership with Gurus and parents organized a very interactive hour of games like lemon and spoon race, 3 legged race and a cricket match using a clip board as a bat. The involvement and joy and laughter was very refreshing to see and be apart of. Hugh thanks to the Young Adults for organizing this happy day. A very special celebration was held upstairs by the ladies, a baby shower for Sister Viktoria. All the ladies sang bhajans and offered gifts to welcome the new member of our Edmonton Sai Family. This was followed by a very elevating Bhajan session and a delicious lunch prepared by our loving parents.

Happy Father's day to our beloved Swami and all Fathers and grandfathers. May this special day fill you with overwhelming love and hugs.

Looking ahead.

Our SSSE Students and Gurus are bringing their 2023- 2024 year to a close. We thank all the Gurus, Parents and Students for their lovely participation in all of our Center programs like monthly Bhajan singing, Birthday, Aradhana, Walk 4 Values and Seva to name a few. Enjoy your summer vacation. To our graduates. Congratulations to you and your parents for successfully completing the SSSE program. We pray that you spend some time reflecting on the learnings and disciplines you have received and add this to your life as a daily practice as it will give you strength and patience as you journey through life. We pray that you continue to find time to be apart of our Center activities or maybe become a Guru. How wonderful this would be.

"When you take one step to Me, I will take one hundred steps to you." Baba

Have a wonderful week. Sairam,

Troy
