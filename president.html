From President's Pen ...

Sai Ram!

Today, when humanity is facing all sorts of tragedies from political turmoil to natural disasters of earthquakes and wildfires, it is crucial that we promote dear Swami’s teaching of love and compassion in society. Swami once said, “The world has to be brought back to the rails. Only love and peace can achieve this. Fill your thoughts, speech, and actions with love, truth, and peace, and engage yourselves in service actions. We aspire for peace and comfort all the time, but where can we find it? Is it to be found in the material world around us? Experience shows that the peace or happiness got from external objects is not enduing.” Swami further adds, "Peace is something which swells from within you. It is not something which is gathered from outside.” You may also recall dear Swami’s famous quote, “If there is righteousness in the heart, there will be beauty in the character. If there is beauty in the character, there will be harmony in the home. If there is harmony in the home, there will be order in the nations. When there is order in the nations, there will be peace in the world.”

With these Swami’s teachings in mind, let us join one of the most important functions of the year, Walk for Values, which originated in Edmonton and now has become a worldwide event. ‘Global Harmony Begins with ME’ is a very timely theme for this year’s Walk. The festivities will start at 10:30 am at Dr. Wilbert McIntyre Park, 8331 - 104 Street. Besides various entertainment and refreshments, we will also have a distinguished individual, Jananee Rasiah, the Associate Dean of the Faculty of Nursing at Grant MacEwan University, as a guest speaker. So please join with family and friends and enjoy the Walk.

Preparations are in full swing for the Western Canada Retreat (with Sai). The retreat will be a great experience for adults as well as the SSE students who will enjoy the superb nature in a Sai environment. To give you a sneak preview of the program, arrangements are being made for musical concerts, devotional singing sessions, healthy living workshops, canoeing, and various sports. So, please reserve the dates of August 25-August 28 this year and register as soon as possible if you have not done so.

I extend my grateful thanks to all the volunteers who have come forward to help with the above mentioned special events. Some of these special members have been coming to the Centre for cleaning and maintenance on a regular basis throughout the year. Swami always said, "Hands that help are holier than lips that pray".

Finally, please join me in our continued prayers for World peace.

With loving Sairam,

Prem Kharbanda
