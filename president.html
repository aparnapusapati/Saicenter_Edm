From President's Pen ...

Sai Ram!

My humble Pranams to our beloved Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba. "Being a good example, is the best form of Service." Baba "Be the change you want to see in the world." Mahatma Gandhi

I must express my heart felt love and gratitude to everyone for their participation in our tree planting exercise organized by Brother Somesh. It filled my heart with so much joy to see our SSSE students and parents, the young and the young at heart, planting trees side by side as captured by Aarti in her short video clip. I must confess, sometimes I think our kids and us as adults, are so carried away with all the numerous distractions that surround us mostly by the ease of access to info and funny clips and videos and games thru technology that we loose our connection to the actual world we live in. The balance of work/ study and play is so out of sync. So it was, to me, a wonderful moment shared.

I truly look forward to the next 2 Fridays we have planned for tree planting. I hope that others will join us this Friday for an evening of tree planting and building of wonderful memories with family and friends.

Have a wonderful week. Sairam,

Troy
