THOUGHT FOR THE WEEK
Love for Love's sake; do not manifest it for the sake of material objects or for the fulfillment of worldly desires. Desire begets anger, anger provokes sin, and under its impact, friends are seen as foes. Anger is at the bottom of every variety of calamity. Therefore, do not fall prey to it. Treat everyone, whoever they may be with the all-inclusive compassion of Love. This constructive empathy has to become the spontaneous reaction of all mankind. Saturate your every breath, while you inhale and while you exhale, with Love. Saturate each moment with Love. Love knows no fear. Love shuns falsehood. Fear drags man into falsehood, injustice and wrong. Love does not crave for praise; that is its strength. Only those who have no Love in them seek reward and reputation. The reward for Love is Love itself. Selfless love is the highest duty, the noblest Godliness.
- Divine Discourse, Jul 29, 1969
You worship with faith and you experience Grace. Faith results in Grace, without you being aware of it. - Baba

From President's Pen ...

Sairam: Now that the 2nd phase of the Alberta provincial economic recovery has been launched, rec-centers and swimming pools are open with some restrictions, and cap on number of people in gatherings at places of worship has been relaxed, one would think that the threat due to COVID-19 might have gone down! I think, on the contrary, the risk of infection from the virus has gone up due to the increased social activity; we have to be twice as much vigilant about the threat and take extra precautions.

With that in mind, should we open the Center for one day on Guru Poornima? The executives deliberated on this issue seriously over the past few days and came to the conclusion that considering the recent surge in COVID-19 cases in Edmonton, it is not worth the risk. So, reluctantly, we cannot open the center for live bhajans and programs until the pandemic situation improves.

In the meanwhile, let us join the SSIO first-ever Online Global Guru Poornima celebration on 4th and 5th July 2020. There is a three-part series of short study guides, encapsulating some of Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba's Divine Discourses regarding Guru Poornima. Please access the program at: https://lists.sathyasai.org/links/6bjHAaqpw/FE9Z8opNv/3o43Lh_xcKl/XwFVQLgwC2

Walk for Values was great and had a good participation. Several of our members had an organized walk at the Terwillegar Park and many others ‘Walked’ in their own neighborhood. We also much appreciate participation of several members of the Mahatma Gandhi Foundation in their own sectors in Edmonton and Calgary.

Ladies wing has undertaken an excellent project of sewing face masks. Under the leadership of Indira, they have made 108 good quality, washable cloth masks for the Center; they are looking into how to distribute these. We also appreciate a gift of 35 well-designed face masks from Terrie and Jitendra Shah on behalf of the Mahatma Gandhi Foundation; their gift is greatly admired!

Center was professionally cleaned, rugs shampooed, and disinfected. Thanks to Brother TR Pillay, and Larry for looking after it, and to all the volunteers, including several youth members for the help arranging chairs and furniture.

At this time of the year, close to Guru Poornima, most devotees have performed some Seva activity at the Operation Friendship Seniors Society (OFSS). Due to the restrictions brought by the pandemic, that activity is not possible; the OFSS would much appreciate receiving monetary donations instead. If you would like to participate, please consider making donation as outlined in an appeal from OFSS in this newsletter.

Service at Operation Friendship Seniors Society

Sairam Devotees: We hope that all of you are safe and well. As a Seva project, our Center has regularly done the lunch preparation and serving to seniors at Operation Friendship Seniors Society (OFSS). Services at OFSS have been a very satisfying and humbling experience for our members for past number of years. As you will appreciate because of current ongoing COVID-19 restrictions, group service is restricted and has not been possible since March 7th when we had our last community seva there.

At the request of OFSS this year, we plan to do a monetary donation drive for OFSS on the occasion of Guru Purnima (July 5th), similar to the successful drive done for Edmonton Food Bank. We are appealing for VOLUNTARY donations by Interac-Money Transfer.

If you wish to participate in this Seva project and support this most worthy cause in this uncertain economic times when the demand on OFSS services is high, please do so by taking the following easy steps of internet banking:

1.Access your bank account on your mobile phone or computer

2.Make an Interac Bank Transfer for whatever amount you wish to donate to: PREM KHARBANDA by email. His email address is: premkharbanda@gmail.com

In the message box, please type: DONATION TO OFSS and your POSTAL ADDRESS (needed for a tax receipt).

Note:

1. There is no need for a secret answer because uncle Prem has an Auto deposit account and there are no bank charges.

2. If you prefer mailing a donation by cheque, please do so. Address: Sathya Sai Baba Centre of Edmonton 9619-82nd Ave NW Edmonton T6C 0Z9

3.Kindly make your donations by July 11th, 2020. Thank you for your support of this worthy cause. Hope you will enjoy the Guru Purnima activities planned for this year.
