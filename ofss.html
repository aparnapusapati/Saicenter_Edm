Sairam Devotees: We hope that all of you are safe and well. As a Seva project, our Center has regularly done the lunch preparation and serving to seniors at Operation Friendship Seniors Society (OFSS). Services at OFSS have been a very satisfying and humbling experience for our members for past number of years. As you will appreciate because of current ongoing COVID-19 restrictions, group service is restricted and has not been possible since March 7th when we had our last community seva there.

At the request of OFSS this year, we plan to do a monetary donation drive for OFSS on the occasion of Guru Purnima (July 5th), similar to the successful drive done for Edmonton Food Bank. We are appealing for VOLUNTARY donations by Interac-Money Transfer.

If you wish to participate in this Seva project and support this most worthy cause in this uncertain economic times when the demand on OFSS services is high, please do so by taking the following easy steps of internet banking:

1.Access your bank account on your mobile phone or computer

2.Make an Interac Bank Transfer for whatever amount you wish to donate to: PREM KHARBANDA by email. His email address is: premkharbanda@gmail.com

In the message box, please type: DONATION TO OFSS and your POSTAL ADDRESS (needed for a tax receipt).

Note:

1. There is no need for a secret answer because uncle Prem has an Auto deposit account and there are no bank charges.

2. If you prefer mailing a donation by cheque, please do so. Address: Sathya Sai Baba Centre of Edmonton 9619-82nd Ave NW Edmonton T6C 0Z9

3. Kindly make your donations by July 11th, 2020. Thank you for your support of this worthy cause. Hope you will enjoy the Guru Purnima activities planned for this year.
